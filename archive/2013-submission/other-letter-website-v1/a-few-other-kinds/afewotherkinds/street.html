An exploration of ethnic divide in graphical elements of a street.

The Street

Explore how ethnic divide is created in a street with graphic elements, and try to blur them out.

The second phase of the project uses the developed hybrid writing system in an urban street which has a high Tamil population density and tries to make the street more familiar to a Sinhala audience. Street signage, shop names, and other typographic elements are reimagined using the hybrid letterforms.
